## The Challenge

Finova's wealth management platform served thousands of financial advisors, but the aging interface was causing errors, slowing workflows, and driving users to competitor products. The platform needed a modern redesign that maintained familiarity while dramatically improving usability and efficiency.

## Our Approach

Financial platforms are uniquely complex — they handle sensitive data, require regulatory compliance, and serve users with deep domain expertise. We conducted extensive shadowing sessions with financial advisors, watching them navigate the existing platform to understand their real workflows, shortcuts, and frustrations.

> "Most redesigns in fintech fail because designers don't understand the domain. This team invested the time to truly learn our users' world before touching a single screen."

## The Solution

We redesigned the platform with a focus on workflow efficiency and error prevention:

- **Contextual dashboards** that surface the most relevant information based on the advisor's current task
- **Smart forms** with inline validation, auto-fill suggestions, and clear error recovery paths
- **A modular layout system** that advisors can customize to match their personal workflow
- **A comprehensive design system** with 300+ components built for the financial services context

## The Results

The redesigned platform transformed Finova's competitive position. Advisors reported that common tasks took significantly less time, and the dramatic reduction in user errors directly improved client satisfaction and regulatory compliance. The modular design system also enabled Finova's internal team to ship new features independently.
