## The Challenge

LaunchPad, an early-stage startup accelerator, needed a website and brand identity that would attract top-tier startups to their program. Their existing site was generic, lacked personality, and failed to communicate the unique value of their accelerator.

## Our Approach

We started with a brand workshop to define LaunchPad's positioning, personality, and visual direction. We then conducted competitor research to understand how other accelerators presented themselves, identifying opportunities for differentiation.

> "They helped us articulate what makes us different — and then translated that into a visual identity that feels authentically us."

## The Solution

We delivered a comprehensive brand and web experience:

- **A bold visual identity** with a custom color palette, typography system, and illustration style that captures LaunchPad's energetic startup culture
- **A conversion-optimized website** with strategically placed social proof, clear CTAs, and an application funnel designed to qualify leads
- **Custom micro-animations** that bring the brand to life and create memorable moments throughout the user journey
- **A brand guidelines document** ensuring consistency across all future touchpoints

## The Results

The new website and brand identity immediately impacted LaunchPad's ability to attract and convert high-quality startup applicants. The improved messaging and design created a professional, trustworthy impression that resonated with their target audience.
