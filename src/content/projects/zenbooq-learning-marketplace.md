## The Challenge

Zenbooq's learning marketplace had thousands of courses but learners couldn't find the right ones. Course pages lacked consistency, the learning experience felt disconnected, and completion rates were alarmingly low. Instructors were also frustrated with the course creation tools.

## Our Approach

We conducted a full UX audit and analyzed behavioral data from over 100,000 learner sessions. We interviewed both learners and instructors to understand both sides of the marketplace, mapping out the complete journey from course discovery to certification.

> "The redesign transformed our platform. Learners stay longer, complete more courses, and instructors tell us the new creation tools are a joy to use."

## The Solution

We overhauled the entire marketplace experience for both learners and instructors:

- **Personalized learning paths** powered by skill assessments and career goals
- **Immersive course player** with distraction-free mode, progress tracking, and interactive notes
- **Smart search and filters** with topic tags, difficulty levels, and peer ratings
- **Instructor dashboard** with analytics, audience insights, and a streamlined course builder
- **Accessibility-first design** ensuring the platform works for learners of all abilities

## The Results

The redesigned marketplace saw dramatic improvements across every key metric. Course completion rates jumped 80%, driven by the new immersive learning experience and progress tracking. The improved discovery system helped learners find relevant courses 50% faster, and instructor satisfaction soared thanks to the new creation tools.
