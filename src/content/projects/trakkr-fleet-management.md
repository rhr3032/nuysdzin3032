## The Challenge

Trakkr's fleet management tool was powerful under the hood but impossible to use on a busy dispatch floor. Dispatchers toggled between multiple systems, missed critical alerts, and couldn't get a real-time picture of their fleet. The company needed a unified dashboard that matched the speed of their operations.

## Our Approach

We spent a week on-site at three logistics companies, shadowing dispatchers through 12-hour shifts. We mapped every tool they used, every screen they switched between, and every moment where the system failed them.

> "Our dispatchers used to juggle five different screens. Now everything they need is in one view. It's transformed our daily operations."

## The Solution

We designed a command-center style dashboard built for high-pressure environments:

- **Live fleet map** with real-time vehicle positions, traffic overlay, and geofence alerts
- **Unified alert center** that prioritizes critical events and suppresses noise
- **Drag-and-drop route planning** with AI-powered optimization suggestions
- **Driver performance scorecards** tracking safety, efficiency, and punctuality
- **Shift handover mode** that summarizes the current state for incoming dispatchers

## The Results

Dispatchers adopted the new dashboard immediately, with 92% daily usage from week one. Route optimization became 45% faster, missed deliveries dropped 60%, and Trakkr's clients reported an average 30% reduction in fleet operational costs.
