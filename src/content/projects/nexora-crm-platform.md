## The Challenge

Nexora's sales teams were drowning in data. Their existing CRM was cluttered with unused features, manual data entry consumed hours each day, and reps couldn't quickly identify which leads deserved attention. The result was missed opportunities and frustrated teams.

## Our Approach

We embedded with three different sales teams for two weeks, observing their daily workflows and documenting every friction point. We conducted 25 in-depth interviews with sales reps, managers, and executives to understand the full spectrum of needs.

> "They didn't just redesign our CRM — they reimagined how our sales team works. The AI suggestions actually feel helpful, not intrusive."

## The Solution

We designed a CRM experience that works with salespeople, not against them:

- **AI-powered lead scoring** that surfaces the most promising opportunities automatically
- **Smart inbox** that consolidates emails, calls, and tasks into a single prioritized view
- **Automated data capture** that eliminates manual entry by pulling information from emails and meetings
- **Visual pipeline management** with drag-and-drop deal stages and real-time forecasting
- **Contextual insights** that provide relevant talking points before every customer interaction

## The Results

Within three months of launch, Nexora's sales teams reported a dramatic shift in how they work. Reps spend 55% more time selling and 70% less time on administrative tasks. The AI-powered lead scoring alone helped teams close 30% more deals in the first quarter.
