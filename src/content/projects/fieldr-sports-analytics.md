## The Challenge

Fieldr had rich performance data but coaches couldn't act on it in real time. The existing tool required data science expertise to extract insights, game-day analysis was too slow, and scouting reports took days to compile. Coaches needed a platform that spoke their language, not a statistician's.

## Our Approach

We shadowed coaching staffs across four professional sports teams during game days and training sessions. We mapped out decision-making moments where data could change outcomes and identified the exact visualizations coaches needed at each point.

> "For the first time, I can see patterns during the game and adjust tactics on the fly. It's like having a data analyst sitting right next to me on the bench."

## The Solution

We designed an analytics platform built for the speed of sport:

- **Live game dashboard** with real-time performance metrics and tactical heat maps
- **Play-by-play breakdown** with video sync and annotation tools for instant review
- **Scouting hub** with automated opponent analysis and tendency reports
- **Player development tracker** with longitudinal performance trends and injury risk indicators
- **Presentation mode** for team meetings with coach-friendly visualizations and highlights

## The Results

Fieldr was adopted by 15 professional teams within the first season. Coaches reported 80% faster post-game analysis, and the scouting hub reduced report compilation time from days to hours. Teams using the platform saw an average 22% improvement in win rates, attributing gains to faster, data-informed decision-making.
