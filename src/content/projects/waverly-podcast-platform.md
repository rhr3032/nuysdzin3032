## The Challenge

Waverly wanted to enter the crowded podcast market with a differentiated experience. Existing platforms were cluttered, discovery was broken, and listeners struggled to find shows they'd love. Waverly needed a platform that made podcast discovery as effortless as music streaming.

## Our Approach

We surveyed 2,000 podcast listeners and conducted deep-dive interviews with 50 power users to understand listening habits, discovery preferences, and pain points with existing platforms.

> "They nailed the discovery experience. I'm finding podcasts I never would have stumbled on before — the personalized recommendations feel genuinely curated."

## The Solution

We created a listening experience built around discovery and engagement:

- **Mood-based discovery** that recommends podcasts based on activity, time of day, and listening history
- **Episode previews** with 30-second audio snippets for quick sampling before committing
- **Smart playlists** that auto-generate episode queues based on interests and available listening time
- **Social listening** features letting users share clips and see what friends are listening to
- **Creator tools** with audience analytics and engagement metrics

## The Results

Waverly launched to strong adoption, hitting 500K monthly active users within six months. The mood-based discovery feature became the most-used entry point, and average session length exceeded industry benchmarks by 68%.
