## The Challenge

ShopVerse had strong products and competitive pricing, but their online shopping experience was losing customers at every stage of the funnel — from product discovery to checkout. Analytics showed high bounce rates, significant cart abandonment, and poor mobile conversion.

## Our Approach

We conducted a comprehensive UX audit combined with user behavior analysis using heatmaps, session recordings, and user interviews. We identified the specific friction points causing drop-offs and prioritized them based on business impact.

> "They showed us exactly where we were losing customers and why. The data-driven approach made it easy to justify the investment internally."

## The Solution

We redesigned the shopping experience with a focus on reducing friction and increasing average order value:

- **Personalized product discovery** with smart filters, visual search, and AI-powered recommendations
- **Streamlined checkout** reduced from 5 steps to 2, with saved payment methods and address auto-complete
- **Enhanced product pages** with better imagery, size guides, and social proof placement
- **Cross-sell modules** strategically placed throughout the journey to increase basket size

## The Results

The redesigned experience transformed ShopVerse's e-commerce metrics across the board. The simplified checkout and improved product discovery created a shopping experience that customers enjoyed, leading to higher conversion and significantly increased order values.
